Summary

"Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a skilled thief who is capable of entering the dreams of others to steal their secrets. However, he is tasked with planting an idea into the mind of a CEO through the process of "inception".

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Marion Cotillard.
