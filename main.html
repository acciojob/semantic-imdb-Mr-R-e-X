Summary

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a drama film directed by Frank Darabont, based on a novella by Stephen King. The story follows Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, even though he claims he is innocent.

Actors

The movie stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding.
